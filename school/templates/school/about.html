About Thuto Thebe Secondary School

Thuto Thebe Secondary School is located in Standerton, Mpumalanga, approximately 5km from town. The school was established in 1995 and offers education from Grade 8 to Grade 12.

Vision

Providing quality education through high quality teaching and training in a safe environment.

Mission

We as a community of Thuto Thebe Secondary School are committed to motivating and encouraging our learners to be highly effective adults who will face life challenges in a positive manner.
